DjangoCon Europe
In Copenhagen
April 10-12th 2019

After a lot of planning, we are happy to announce that we'll be hosting all you lovely Django people in Copenhagen this year!

You don't necessarily need to buy plane tickets or book hotel rooms yet: Alternatives such as ride sharing and accommodation sharing will be provided through the website for interested ticket-holders from December and onward.

Here's our plan:

October: Early organizing of the conference (volunteers, get in touch!) ✔️

November: More organizing, website is booted with a CfP ✔️

December: Ticket sale opens, including info on scholarships

January: Early bird/Sharing Pioneer discount expires

January 13th: CfP closes

March: Talks start being published

April: Main conference (10-12th), sprints (13-14th) and opportunities to do workshops before and after
